Website Management

A website is a long-term investment that requires ongoing attention to remain secure and effective. Our website management services are designed to handle the technical details, so you can focus on your business. We provide proactive monitoring and maintenance to ensure your site runs smoothly and safely.

Comprehensive Website Care

Our management plans provide peace of mind and keep your online presence professional and secure. We handle the essential, ongoing tasks required for a healthy website.

  • Proactive Security Monitoring: We keep your website secure by continuously monitoring for threats and ensuring all software, including plugins and themes, is kept up-to-date.
  • Safe & Tested Updates: To prevent disruptions, all updates are carefully tested in a staging environment before being applied to your live website. This ensures compatibility and protects your site's functionality.
  • Ongoing SEO Management: For WordPress sites, we offer ongoing SEO services to help improve your visibility in search results. This builds upon the foundational SEO included in our web design packages.
  • Performance & Analytics: We monitor your website's performance and can provide analytics reports upon request, giving you valuable insights into your visitors and site health.

Website Rescue & Takeover

Have an existing website that has been neglected or needs fixing? We can help. We will assess your current site's condition and, if we can assist, propose a plan to get it back in top shape. There is no charge for the initial assessment if we determine we cannot help.

Flexible Support & Pricing

Our management plans are billed monthly and are tailored to the services you need. We believe in supporting our clients, which is why our plans include complimentary assistance for minor tasks and quick questions.

For more substantial work that requires dedicated time, such as significant content additions or design changes, we provide a separate, transparent quote upfront. This approach ensures you only pay for what you need while still having access to support for small requests.
