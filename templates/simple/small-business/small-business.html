Our Story

Welcome to The Daily Grind! We're more than just a coffee shop; we're a community hub. It all started with a simple love for coffee and a desire to create a warm, inviting space for friends and neighbors to gather.

We proudly serve locally roasted, fair-trade coffee and source our ingredients from local bakeries and farms whenever possible. Every cup is brewed with care, and every pastry is baked with love. Come in, relax, and make yourself at home.

The Perfect Roast

Our passion starts with the bean. We partner with ethical growers to source the highest quality, single-origin beans. Each batch is roasted in-house to unlock its unique flavor profile, resulting in a truly exceptional cup of coffee every time.

More Than a Coffee Shop

We're a gathering place for our neighbors. A spot to work, to chat, to create, and to connect. We proudly feature local artists on our walls and host open mic nights every Friday. Come for the coffee, stay for the community.
